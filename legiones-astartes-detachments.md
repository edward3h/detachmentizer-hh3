# Legiones Astartes Detachments

* All of these detachments have the Legiones Astartes faction.
* Centurion is a Command unit with the Officer of the Line (2) rule.
  * Includes other units with "Centurion" as a word in a longer name.

## Auxiliary Detachments

### Daemonic Manifestation

* Traitor only.
* Unlocked by unit Esoterist.
* Slots in this Detachment may only
be used to select Ruinstorm Daemon Brute Units
from the Daemons of the Ruinstorm faction.
* Slots:
  * 3 Heavy Assault

### Veteran Cadre

* Unlocked by unit Legion Champion.
* Slots:
  * 1 Retinue
  * 1 Elites
  * 1 Heavy Transport

### Apothecarion Delegation

* Slots in this Detachment may only be used
to select Apothecary Units.
* Slots:
  * 6 Support. 1 is Prime.

### Techmarine Covenant

### Recon Demi-company

## Apex Detachments

### Medusan Vanguard

* Sub-faction: Iron Hands
* Unlocked by unit Iron Father _or_ Ferrus Manus
* The Command Slot in this Detachment may only
be used to select a Praevian Unit.
* Slots:
  * 1 Command. 1 is Prime.
  * 2 Heavy Assault
  * 1 Support
  * 1 War-Engine

### The Hammer of Olympia

* Sub-faction: Iron Warriors
* Unlocked by unit Warsmith _or_ Perturabo
* Slots:
  * 2 Heavy Assault. 1 is Prime.
  * 1 Heavy Transport
  * 2 Troops. 1 is Prime.

### Atramentar Hunt

* Sub-faction: Night Lords
* Retinue Slots in this Detachment must be filled
with Cataphractii Command Squad Units or
Tartaros Command Squad Units.
* Heavy Assault Slots in this Detachment must be
filled with Cataphractii Terminator Squad Units or
Tartaros Terminator Squad Units.
* The only Prime rule that can be used in this detachment is **Atramentar**
* Slots:
  * 1 Retinue. 1 is Prime.
  * 2 Heavy Assault. 2 are Prime.

### Exalted Conclave

* Sub-faction: Word Bearers
* Traitor only
* The only Prime rule that can be used in this detachment is **True Believers**
* Slots:
  * 2 Troops. 2 are Prime.
  * 2 Elites

## Brotherhood of the Phoenix

* Sub-faction: Emperor's Children
* Traitor allegiance only.
* Only if Primary Detachment sub-faction is Emperor's Children.
* A Warlord detachment may not be taken if this detachment is taken.
* High Command and Command units in this detachment do not unlock any other detachments.
* This Detachment must include a Fulgrim Transfigured Unit. (Warlord)
* Slots:
  * 1 Warlord
  * 3 High Command. 1 is Prime.
  * 1 Command.
